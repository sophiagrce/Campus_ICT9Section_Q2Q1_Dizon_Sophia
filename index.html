TYPES OF CRYPTO CURRENCIES

Bitcoin


Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that allows peer-to-peer transactions over the internet without the need for intermediaries like banks. It operates on a technology called blockchain, which is a secure, transparent ledger that records all transactions. Bitcoin is created through a process called mining and is known for its volatility, limited supply, and potential as a store of value or speculative investment.

Solana


Solana is a high-performance blockchain designed to support decentralized applications (dApps) and cryptocurrencies. Known for its fast transaction speeds and low fees, Solana uses a unique consensus mechanism called Proof of History (PoH) alongside Proof of Stake (PoS), enabling it to process thousands of transactions per second. This scalability makes Solana a popular choice for developers building decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms, NFT projects, and other blockchain-based applications.

Binance Coin (BNB)


Binance Coin (BNB) is the native cryptocurrency of the Binance exchange, one of the largest cryptocurrency platforms in the world. Originally launched as a utility token to pay for reduced trading fees, BNB has since expanded its use across Binance's ecosystem, including for transactions on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, and even for real-world purchases. BNB is designed to be fast, low-cost, and highly versatile within the Binance ecosystem.
